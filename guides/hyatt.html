World of Hyatt — Complete Guide

Overview

World of Hyatt is Hyatt’s global loyalty program spanning luxury and lifestyle brands (Park Hyatt, Andaz, Grand Hyatt, Hyatt Regency, Hyatt Place, Hyatt House, and more). Members earn points on eligible room charges and select incidentals, and can redeem points for free nights, room upgrades, dining, Spa & Experiences, and more.

World of Hyatt — Key Concepts

  • Eligible charges: qualifying room rate, taxes, and eligible incidentals (dining, spa at participating hotels).
  • Base earning: points per USD spent on eligible charges (varies by rate and promotions).
  • Points+Cash: Mixed redemptions allow partial points + cash bookings.
How to Earn Points
  • Stay at Hyatt properties and provide your World of Hyatt number at booking / check-in.
  • Use co-branded cards or transfer partners where available (country dependent).
  • Participate in Hyatt promotions (bonus points, extra elite nights).
  • Book experiences (e.g., dining, spa) at participating Hyatt hotels charged to your room.
How to Redeem Points
  • Book standard award nights by category or dynamic pricing.
  • Use points for upgrades, dining, spa, and Hyatt experiences.
  • Consider Points + Cash for flexibility when short on points.

Typical transfer rule (where available): often 5 World of Hyatt points → 2 airline miles, minimum conversion 5,000 points, and conversions in increments of 1,250 points. Some promotions add a bonus (e.g., 5,000-mile bonus when converting 50,000+ points). Always verify the exact ratio and minimum on the official World of Hyatt partner page before initiating transfers.

Points Pooling & Family Use

Hyatt supports options to share and pool points in many programs (check account settings). Practical points:

  • Household pooling: some family members can combine points for redemptions (subject to account rules).
  • Book on behalf of others: you can redeem points for stays for friends/family using your account.
  • Gift nights and certificates may be available through promotions or status rewards.

Always verify the official World of Hyatt terms for eligibility and any regional restrictions.
